A Court of Mist and Fury

"A Court of Mist and Fury" is a deeply immersive and emotionally resonant novel that will captivate readers with its rich storytelling and multifaceted characters.

The Gathering

"Soon, we may live in a world where they are nothing more than a legend." The Gathering is a chilling murder mystery set against the stark backdrop of small-town Alaska, where the murder of a teenager sets off a chain reaction of violence and prejudice against vampires.
